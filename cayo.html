Payout Formula

Gross loot = Primary (×1.1 if Hard Mode) + optimal secondary fill across all player bags + $59,500 office safe (avg).

Net distributable = Gross − 10% fencing − 2% Pavel = Gross × 0.88.

Host cut = 100% − (15% × number of other players). Each player starts at 15%, adjustable in 5% increments, minimum 15%.

Elite Challenge adds +$50k/player (normal) or +$100k/player (hard) — applied after the split, not subject to fees. Only if all bags are full.

Bag Optimizer Logic

Bags filled greedily by value density ($ per full bag): Gold ($496k) → Cocaine ($401k) → Weed ($354k) → Painting ($338k) → Cash ($324k).

Paintings cannot be taken partially — skipped if remaining space < 50%.

Bags allocated host-first (best bag to host). Each bag filled independently from remaining stacks. Greedy sequential fill maximizes total secondary value.

Secondary values are session averages. Actual payouts may vary ±5%.
