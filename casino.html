Man-Seconds Budget Model

Total man-seconds = vault time × player count. This is the budget for all activities in the vault.

Hacking cost = per hacker: doors_assigned × (hacks_per_door × seconds_per_hack + animation_per_door). Hackers work in parallel; each hacker's time is subtracted individually from the man-seconds pool (not wall-clock).

Animation time = 24s per door on normal, 48s per door on hard. Hacks per door: 1 on normal, 2 on hard. These values are placeholders pending community verification.

Door assignment = 4 doors split across active hackers. Fastest hacker gets extra doors when they don't divide evenly.

Commute time = number_of_piles × seconds_per_pile (default 10s). Flat total deducted from available man-seconds.

Collection = remaining man-seconds ÷ seconds per unit, floored, capped at max units available.

Crew deductions = gross × (hacker% + gunman% + driver%). Net to players = gross − deductions.
