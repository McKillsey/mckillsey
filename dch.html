Man-Seconds Budget Model

Total man-seconds = vault time × player count.

Hacking cost = per hacker: doors × (hacks_per_door × s/hack + animation_per_door). Parallel across hackers; each subtracted individually from man-seconds pool.

Animation time = 24s/door normal, 28s/door hard. Hacks per door: 1 normal, 2 hard. Placeholders pending community verification.

Door assignment = 4 doors across active hackers. Fastest gets extra doors when uneven.

Commute = piles × s/pile (default 5). Flat total from man-seconds.

Collection = remaining man-seconds ÷ s/unit, floored, capped at max.

Crew = gross × (hacker% + gunman% + driver% + Lester 5%).

Laundering fee = 10% of gross for low buyer, 5% for medium, 0% for high. Deducted after crew.
