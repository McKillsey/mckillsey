Sell Missions

Six missions are possible, drawn at random. Vehicle count scales with stock level — 0–25% spawns 1 vehicle (solo-able), 25–50% spawns 2, 50–75% spawns 3, 75–100% spawns up to 4. At full stock that means 3–4 vehicles and a 15-minute time limit. Completing that solo is genuinely hard: high execution risk under time pressure, and in a public lobby you add grief risk on top. McKillsey standard is 3 helpers — one per vehicle, one on security — for a reason.

McKillsey Verdicts
The Bunker in Context

The Bunker generates the highest single-sell net income of any passive business we track — $1,200,000 per sell with the lobby bonus applied. The $/active-hr at crew + full lobby is $1,800,000, after accounting for the three helpers you owe 30 minutes back. The constraint is time: nearly 12 hours per fill cycle means this is a multi-session asset, not a grind loop. Set it, let it fill, sell when crew is available.

Solo + Full Lobby shows $3,600,000/active-hr on paper — highest of any scenario on this page — because there is no reciprocity cost. But that number comes with two compounding risks. Execution risk: delivering 3–4 vehicles to separate drop-offs solo within a 15-minute window is genuinely difficult, and any failed delivery means restarting. Grief risk: doing all of this with $1.2M of cargo in a public lobby, where any other player can attack and reset the mission. That rate is real under ideal conditions. Most players cannot reliably create ideal conditions.

Why 3 Helpers

At full stock, the Bunker spawns up to 4 vehicles depending on the mission. Three helpers means one person per extra vehicle while you manage a fourth or run security. Two can work for specific missions, but the McKillsey standard is 3 for full stock — it ensures no mission catches you shorthanded. Total active cost: 10 min sell + (3 × 10 min reciprocity) = 40 min.

On Resupply

Always buy. Five steal missions to save $375,000 costs more in active time than the money saved — and production halts entirely during steal missions, compounding the loss further. Buy, wait, sell. The Bunker rewards patience, not hustle.

Bunker Location

Location affects mission viability more than any other variable. The Farmhouse bunker (Blaine County) is significantly more practical than Paleto Bay for most missions — shorter delivery routes and fewer obstacle-heavy terrain sections. If you haven't purchased yet, Farmhouse is the McKillsey pick.
