2× Counterfeit Cash Sell Missions
/cash · /mc
The headline this week. Cash jumps from second-best MC business to comfortably the most profitable sell mission in the entire GTA Online universe. With the Car Wash front bonus stacking on top of 2×, a crew sell in a full lobby nets $1,338,420 per fill — that's $2,676,840/active hour, more than double what Coke earns in a normal week.

If you own Cash: run it every fill. If you don't, factories are 40% off this week. If you own the mansion's Car Wash front business, the +35% bonus stacks multiplicatively with the 2× — this is exactly the week it was made for.
If you own Cash, run as many sales as you can this week.
🤝
2× Street Dealer Sales
/dealers
Our usual advice on street dealers is simple: don't bother. They buy most of your product at roughly supply cost, and the modest profit you can make selling acid or each dealer's premium product is hardly worth the trip. This week is the exception. 2× dealer payouts mean you're earning real margin on every sale. Volume is still limited by dealer cooldowns, but this is the one week where selling to them is defensible.

See /dealers for the full breakdown on which products to prioritize.
Worth your time this week — see /dealers for the math
🏁
3× Lunar New Year Stunt Races
Triple payout on Lunar New Year Stunt Races (6× for GTA+ members). For what it's worth, we think these races are actually pretty fun — but they're not meaningful $/hr relative to actual money-making activities.

If you do run them: the Penumbra FF is the only AWD sports car eligible, making it the easiest experience. The 300R can be faster if you can manage some oversteer.
Fun, not income
🏍️
2× Biker Contracts, MC Work & Challenges
If you're chasing the 50 Clubhouse Contracts needed for the Tier 4 challenge, this is the week to grind them — the 2× RP sweetens an otherwise tedious requirement. For everyone else, the payouts are not competitive with running your actual MC businesses, and the time is better spent elsewhere.
Completionists only

Methodology
How Weekly Bonuses Flow Through Our Numbers

When a 2× sell bonus is active, it applies as a multiplier to the base sale price before the lobby bonus. The full chain is: Base Sale × Front Business Bonus × Weekly Multiplier × Lobby Bonus. All numbers on this page and on affected business pages (/cash, /mc) reflect this stacking. Hover any number with a dashed underline to see the full calculation.

The McKillsey Weekly is updated manually each Thursday when Rockstar resets. If you spot an error, use the Report button — corrections are usually live within hours.
