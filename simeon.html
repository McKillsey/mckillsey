Simeon Didn't Text

Daily Export · Blind Acquisition

Simeon cycles through eight fixed lists of cars. He doesn't always text — but he's never working off a ninth. That structure means you can find a car he wants in at most four attempts, starting from any of four common targets.

Steal a Banshee, Coquette, Infernus, or Sentinel. No two-star wanted level means it wasn't on his list — but you've just eliminated three of the eight possibilities. Work down through the rounds below. Seven times out of eight you'll have a deliver by round three. On the rare third miss, you know exactly which list it is and every car on it.
